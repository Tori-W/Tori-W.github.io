About Me

Hello, my name is Victoria Winn!

I make games, play games, and sing songs.

I'm a 4th year Computer Game Science major at UC Irvine, and the President of the Video Game Development Club at UC Irvine. For 6 years, I've also been an esports competitor in Overwatch — from high school to collegiate to Calling All Heroes.

Fun Facts

  • My first video game was Dynasty Warriors 4.
  • I love singing and karaoke. My go-to karaoke song is "I Will Survive" by Gloria Gaynor.
  • I was in the top 1% of high scores for the Munich map of Mini Motorways
  • My favorite color is orange
